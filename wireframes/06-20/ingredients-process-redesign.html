Implementation notes
IngredientsCard

1. Replace Tabs/TabsList variant="line" with CardTabToggle (already exists at src/components/trials/shared/CardTabToggle.tsx). Move it into the card-header actions row.

2. Replace <IngredientsPieChart> + h-48 container with a horizontal stacked bar: one div with display:flex, each segment a child div with flex: percentage and background color from INGREDIENT_CHART_COLORS. ~10 lines of JSX, no Recharts dependency for display mode.

3. Rename tab option label from "ingredients" to "Formula" to avoid reading the title twice.

ProcessCard

1. Replace the paramProse string join with a <div className="flex flex-wrap gap-1 mt-1"> of chips: each param gets <span className="inline-flex … bg-muted/60 rounded px-1.5 py-0.5 text-[10px]"><span className="font-medium">{p.key}</span><span className="text-muted-foreground ml-1">{p.value}{p.unit}</span></span>.

2. Replace <p className="text-xs text-muted-foreground italic"> for notes with a callout: <div className="flex items-start gap-1.5 mt-1.5 rounded-r bg-amber-50 border-l-2 border-amber-400 px-2 py-1 text-[10px] text-amber-900">. No conditional logic needed — all notes get this treatment.

3. Change step-num from bg-primary/10 text-primary to bg-primary text-primary-foreground (already used in ProcessSheet) for stronger visual anchoring.
