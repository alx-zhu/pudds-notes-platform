Design: Progressive Disclosure Analysis Card (v3)
The card is split into three tiers of information density:
  • Timeline — selects which storage time to view. Active dot is filled + larger with ring. Unselected dots are outline-only (unfilled) for clear distinction.
  • Hero (Photo + Scores) — answers "how good is this pudding?" at a glance. Photo on left (200px, 1:1), scores on right with title + 4-column grid. Clicking photo opens full-size modal (hover shows "View Full Size" overlay).
  • Score Breakdown — collapsible section answers "what drove these scores?" with eval pills and chart. Each eval pill has an inline edit icon.
Actions are co-located with what they modify:
  • Card footer: "+ Add Log" and "Delete Log" (separated, clear hierarchy)
  • Photo: in the hero section next to scores. Click opens full-size photo modal (hover shows overlay). Empty state shows "Add Photo" placeholder.
  • Eval pills: inline edit icon per pill, "+ Add" at end of row
  • Eval delete: inside the edit sheet (not a standalone button)
