Design v4: Stacked Layout
Key insight: photo and scores have fundamentally different aspect ratios. Forcing them side-by-side always creates imbalance at some width.
  • Scores first, photo below. Scores are the hero — full width, prominent. Photo is a wide landscape strip below, supporting visual context.
  • Photo strip — full width within padding, max-height 200px, object-fit cover. Click opens full photo modal. Landscape crop looks good at every width.
  • Works at every viewport — no grid ratio hacks, no fixed pixel widths. Content stacks naturally.
  • Empty photo state — subtle dashed placeholder, shorter height (80px), doesn't waste space.
