Design v7: "Sensory Evaluation" — Photo + Vertical Scores
  • Layout: Same grid structure as current (photo left, content right), but the right half is now the scores instead of the eval chart. This fills the vertical space naturally — overall score is large and centered, category scores stack below as horizontal bars.
  • Renamed: Card title is "Sensory Evaluation" (not "Analysis Logs") to better describe what users are looking at.
  • Progressive disclosure: The detailed per-metric breakdown (chart + eval pills) is hidden behind a collapsible "Score Breakdown" section underneath the hero. Users see the summary first, drill down only if curious.
  • Why this works: The overall score (large centered number) + 3 category bars fill the right column height to roughly match a square photo. No wasted space, no height mismatch.
  • Actions: Footer has log-level actions. Photo click opens photo modal. Eval pill icons handle edit/delete. Clean hierarchy.
